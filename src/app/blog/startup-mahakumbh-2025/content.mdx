From April 3 to 5, 2025, **Startup Mahakumbh**—India's flagship startup gathering—was held at Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi. Organized under the "Startup India @ 2047 – Unfolding the Bharat Story" theme, the event brought together over **3,000 startups**, **1,000 investors**, **10,000 delegates**, and **50,000+ business visitors** from over 50 countries.

## 🚀 Representing West Bengal at the National Stage

I had the honor of representing **NexaWings International Software Solution**—one of the few startups from a West Bengal college—at this national-level platform. It was a privilege to showcase our AI-powered SaaS innovations and workflow automation capabilities among India's top emerging ventures. Our presence underscored the startup talent emerging from West Bengal's academic ecosystem.

This opportunity was particularly meaningful as it highlighted the innovative potential coming from regional educational institutions, proving that groundbreaking ideas can emerge from anywhere in India.

## 🗓️ Event Schedule Overview

- **Dates:** April 3–5, 2025
- **Venue:** Bharat Mandapam, New Delhi  
- **Daily Timings:** 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST
- **Scale:** National platform supported by DPIIT

The three-day event was meticulously organized with thematic pavilions, investor meetups, mentorship sessions, and product showcases across multiple sectors.

## 🎯 Why This Mattered to NexaWings

### National Validation
Participation in a **DPIIT-supported platform** added credibility and recognition to our early-stage venture. Being selected among thousands of applicants validated our innovative approach and business model.

The event provided unprecedented access to:
- **Investors** actively seeking AI and automation solutions
- **Ecosystem enablers** offering scaling support
- **Mentors** with deep industry experience

### Sector Visibility
We showcased our products in the **AI, automation, and SaaS domains**—perfectly aligned with the event's key thematic tracks and pavilions. This positioning helped us connect with the right audience and stakeholders.
